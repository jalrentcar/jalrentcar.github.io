Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I make a booking?

You can book through our website, call us directly, or visit our office. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for best availability.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driving license, passport or citizenship certificate, and a security deposit. International visitors need an International Driving Permit.

Are your vehicles insured?

Yes, all our vehicles are comprehensively insured. We also provide 24/7 roadside assistance for peace of mind during your journey.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking up to 12 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellation charges may apply based on our policy.
